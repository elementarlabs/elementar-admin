Announcement

Introducing the Announcement Component, a simple and effective way to display important messages at the top of your Angular Material app. Whether it’s a critical update, a promotion, or just a friendly reminder, this component ensures your users won’t miss it.

Key Features:
  • ✅ Highly Visible: Always grabs attention without disrupting the user experience.
  • ✅ User-Friendly: Intuitive behavior with dismissible options
  • ✅ Customizable: Easily adapt the design to match your app's style.
  • ✅ Seamless Integration: Built with Angular Material, it fits perfectly into your app.

Keep your users informed with the Announcement Component — the perfect solution for delivering essential updates!
